## What is Apstal?

Apstal is a website analytics platform with a built-in AI chatbot. Instead of reading charts and dashboards, you just ask questions like:

- *"Where do my visitors come from?"*
- *"Which page has the highest bounce rate?"*
- *"How many sales did I get this week?"*

Apstal tracks your visitors, records session replays, and lets you talk to your data using text or voice.

## Quick Start

Add one line to your website:

```html
<script defer src="https://apstal.com/apstal?id=YOUR_PROJECT_ID"></script>
```

Get your free Project ID at [apstal.com](https://apstal.com).

## Features

**AI Chat & Voice**
Ask questions about your analytics in natural language. Type or speak. Apstal responds with real-time insights based on your actual data.

**Session Replay**
Watch how visitors interact with your site. See every click, scroll, and rage click without installing additional tools.

**Cookieless Tracking**
No cookies, no consent banners, no GDPR headaches. Apstal is privacy-first by design.

**One Script, Everything Included**
Analytics, session replay, AI chatbot, and heatmaps in a single lightweight script (~37KB gzipped).

## How It Works

1. You add the Apstal script to your website
2. The script collects anonymous visitor data (page views, clicks, scroll depth, session recordings)
3. Data is sent to Apstal servers for processing
4. You open [apstal.com/chat](https://apstal.com/chat) and ask questions about your traffic

All data is anonymous. No personal information is collected or stored.
